Welcome to Little Jewels School

Little Jewels was founded in 1974 as the first English medium school in response to the need for English medium education. Little Jewels upholds that ‘School is a lighthouse of Society’ and therefore should provide direction and light not only to students but also to parents and the society at large.The School believes that every child is potentially the light of the world.
